Shipping Policy
State laws require someone 21 years of age or older to order online and to sign for the package at delivery. It is preferable to ship to a business address.

Due to constantly changing direct-shipping laws and regulations we cannot ship to every state. This may change at any time. A third-party shipper can send to the following states: AZ, CA, CO, CT, DC, FL, GA, IA, ID, IN, LA, MA, ME, MN, MO, MT, NC, NE, NJ, NM, NV, NY, OH, OR, PA, SC, TX, VT, WA, WV, WY.
If you do not see your state listed, would like to ship internationally or arrange a pick-up or delivery elsewhere than listed, please contact us at info@kohuewines.com to discuss options.
Taxes are calculated at check out. Please note that by purchasing our wine, no matter where you reside, you will be charged a California sales tax. If tax is not calculated at check out, due to entering an address outside of California, you will be billed separately for taxes. Once we receive that payment, we will process order.
You will be notified by email once your wines are shipped. Shipments will begin in November. If you need a rush order, please contact us.
For local pickups, we will contact you to make arrangements.

For any questions, please contact us by emailing info@kohuewines.com
